Who we are

USCA Trading, founded in Canada in 2021, is dedicated to recycling, trading, and exporting with a mission to contribute to a greener planet by minimizing landfill waste. We serve as a comprehensive One Source solution for all recycling requirements.

Each year, we handle over 50,000 metric tonnes of recyclable materials, including waste paper, plastics, and metal scrap, resulting in a reduction of approximately 120,000 tons of CO2 emissions. This not only benefits the environment but also has economic advantages.
